About US

In 2018, under the leadership of Christian Saffon, Jonnathan Garzon, Marcela Gonzales, Jaime Duque and Jhoham Rojas, ADC was born as a company that provides services such as audits and inspections of aeronautical documentation for lessors around the world. In less than two years, the company grew considerably, increasing its portfolio with more robust services for review of unplanned and routine maintenance, scanning and indexing of technical documentation. Currently ADC has a team of highly qualified Aeronautical Engineers with the necessary experience to provide a high-quality product to the client.
